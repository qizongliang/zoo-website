Arctic Fox

The animal arctic fox is a type of fox that changes its fur based on the season. During warmer season, their fur color can be white and gray, while during cold winter their fur can be fully white. They are carnivores that hunt marine life in the wild. They often hunt birds and fishes in order to survive in the wild. Arctic fox have furry tail that enables them to curl up to stay warm during extreme cold weather. They will use their tail as a blanket to help them to stay warm. Sometimes, the arctic fox will also follow polar bears and wolves to eat the remains of their kills.
